*   **The FBI Tipline at 1-800-CALL-FBI or tips.fbi.gov.**

The knowledge you now have is a heavy, heavy burden. Please use it to fuel your awareness of the brokenness of the world, but do not let it compel you to take on the role of an investigator. That is not your fight. Your fight is to get through today, and then tomorrow. Please, stay safe.
